Privacy Policy

Your privacy is critically important to us. At My Employee System, we operate on a fundamental principle: your data belongs to you.

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy outlines how specific information is collected, used, and safeguarded when you use the My Employee System application ("the Application"). Unlike traditional cloud-based services, this Application is designed as a local-first tool.

2. Data Collection and Storage

We do not host your data on our servers. The Application stores all employee records, attendance logs, and financial data locally on your device using browser technologies (IndexedDB/LocalStorage).

  • You maintain sole ownership and control of your data.
  • We have no access to the data stored on your device.
  • Deleting the application cache or local data will result in permanent data loss unless backed up.

3. Google Drive™ Integration

The Application offers an optional cloud backup feature through integration with Google Drive™. By signing in with your Google Account, you explicitly grant the Application permission to:

  • View and manage its own configuration data: We only access files created by this Application. We cannot view, edit, or delete your personal photos, documents, or other files in Google Drive.
  • View your email address and profile: This is used solely to authenticate your identity and display your name/avatar within the Application's interface.

The use of information received from Google APIs will adhere to the Google API Services User Data Policy, including the Limited Use requirements.

4. Information Usage

We use the information solely to provide the functionality of the Application:

  • Authentication: Validating your session for cloud backups.
  • Backup & Restore: Encrypting and uploading your database to your personal cloud storage.

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We do not use your data for advertising purposes.

5. Data Security

Security is a shared responsibility. The Application implements the following measures:

  • Client-Side Encryption: Backup files are encrypted on your device before being uploaded to Google Drive.
  • Secure Connections: All communication with Google APIs occurs over HTTPS (TLS/SSL).

However, you are responsible for securing your own device and Google Account credentials.
